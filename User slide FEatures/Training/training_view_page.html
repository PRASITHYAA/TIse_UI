Workshop Description

This course provides the basic knowledge of protection principles of Power Systems Protection and operations of Numerical relays

>Training Type: Face to Face Training
>Language: English

Who Should Attend

  • Diploma/ Bachelor/ Master Degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineers
  • Protection Technician

Prerequisites

  • Basic Knowledge of the Power System Protection
  • Knowledge in Mathematical calculation
Date & Time / Duration

2 Days Workshop

Location

CHENNAI

Training Schedule

Day 1 :

  • Introduction to power system protection
  • Basic Information and Types of faults
  • Components of the protection system
  • Importance of protection coordination
  • Time scale for protection system
  • Short Circuit calculations
  • Basic principles of power system protections
  • Types of protections – Equipment wise

Day 2 :

  • Settings of power system protections
  • Type of Protection Relays
  • Protection Relays Principle of operation
  • Architecture
  • Communication
  • Numerical relays
  • Testing of Relays
  • Testing of Relays
  • Advantages & disadvantages of Numerical relay
  • Operation & Maintenance of Numerical relays
